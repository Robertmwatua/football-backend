Hello, I'm Robert!

I’m a passionate tech enthusiast, ethical hacker, and developer from Kenya. I love working on smart ideas that help solve everyday problems. This platform is one of my creations to help manage football scores, view league standings, and enjoy the game even more!

A Bit About eFootball

I’ve always enjoyed eFootball because it teaches teamwork, decision-making, and competition. Whether it's on the field or in code, I believe strategy and consistency win games. This system is built for that same spirit — keeping things fair, easy, and fun!

How to Use This Platform

  • First, sign up or log in with your name.
  • Select your favorite teams and save them.
  • Add match results using the Add Match section.
  • Click View Standings to see live updates of the table.
  • Each user's data is saved separately — it’s all yours!
